About This Tour

Somnath and Dwarka are two of Gujarat's most sacred destinations. Somnath is home to the first of the twelve Jyotirlingas, while Dwarka is one of the four sacred dhams of Hinduism and the ancient kingdom of Lord Krishna. Together they form a profound spiritual circuit entirely within Gujarat.

Spectrum Tours & Travels has been running this route since 2008. Whether you're a family of four or a group of fifty, we provide the right vehicle, a verified driver, and a completely hassle-free experience from start to return.

Suggested Itineraries

Option 01

3-Day Express Circuit

  • Day 1: Ahmedabad → Somnath (approx. 5.5 hrs). Evening aarti at Somnath Temple.
  • Day 2: Somnath local sightseeing — Prabhasa Patan Museum, Bhalka Tirth, Triveni Sangam. Depart for Dwarka (3 hrs).
  • Day 3: Dwarka Dwarkadhish Temple darshan, Nageshwar Jyotirlinga, Beyt Dwarka. Return to Ahmedabad.
Option 02 — Recommended

5-Day Relaxed Circuit

  • Includes Gir National Park wildlife safari en route
  • Porbandar — birthplace of Mahatma Gandhi & Sudama Mandir
  • More relaxed darshan timings ideal for elderly pilgrims
  • Optional halt at Sasan Gir for senior-friendly wildlife viewing
