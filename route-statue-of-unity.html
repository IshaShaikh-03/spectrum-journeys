About This Tour

The Statue of Unity, standing tall at 182 meters, is a monumental tribute to the Iron Man of India, Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel. Located on the Sadhu Bet island facing the Narmada Dam, it is approximately 200 kms from Ahmedabad.

Our Ahmedabad to Statue of Unity tour packages are designed to offer maximum comfort and flexibility. Whether you want a quick 1-day sightseeing trip or a relaxed 2-day overnight stay, our fleet of sedans, SUVs, and Tempo Travellers can accommodate groups of all sizes.

Suggested Itineraries

Option 1: Same-Day Return Trip

  • Early morning departure from Ahmedabad (approx. 6:00 AM)
  • Arrive at Kevadia by 9:30 AM
  • Visit Observation Deck, Valley of Flowers, and Sardar Sarovar Dam
  • Evening Projection Mapping Show
  • Return to Ahmedabad by late night

Option 2: 1 Night / 2 Days (Recommended)

  • Relaxed travel schedule with visits to secondary attractions
  • Jungle Safari & Pets Zone exploration
  • Zarwani Waterfall & Poicha Temple visit on the way back
  • Flexible hotel drop-off and pickup in Kevadia
